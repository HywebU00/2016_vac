AL National Commander Dale Barnett and their party visit VAC

  • Date:105/02/04
  • Department:Department of Veterans Service and Assistance
Mr. Dale Barnett, National Commander of the American Legion, Mrs. Sharon Conatser, National President of the American Legion Ladies Auxiliary and their party visited the Veterans Affairs Council (VAC) from November 26th to 30th, 2015. On November 27th , the visitors were given a briefing on the operations of the VAC, which include education assistance, employment assistance, medical care, home care, and services for R.O.C. veterans and their families. Minister Tung expressed his gratitude for The American Legion"s continuing support of Taiwan through its Resolutions at the National Convention each year. Commander Barnett and President Conatser received medals from the VAC in honor of their dedication to veterans" welfare and the promotion of relations between The American Legion and R.O.C. veterans. On the Morning of November 30th, Minister Tung, accompanied the visitors to call on President Ma, Ying-jeou, at President’s Office. President Ma extended a cordial welcome to the distinguished guests and appreciated the American Legion’s efforts on promoting US-ROC relationships on many important occasions, passing resolutions in support of ROC during its annual conventions for many years and supporting arm sales to ROC (Taiwan), according to the “Taiwan Relations Act”. President Ma and visitors also exchanged views on veterans affairs and discussed cross-strait relations. In the end, National Commander Barnett thanked President Ma’s reception and expressed their admiration to VAC for taking good care of veterans. For mutual benefits, National Commander Barnett also pledged to help promote Sino-American bilateral friendships, and the world peace.
